Buatkan sistem ERP berbasis Laravel dengan arsitektur modular, scalable, dan siap produksi untuk sistem sales, admin, driver, inventory, finance, dan CRM.

Sistem ini menggunakan:
- Laravel (backend utama)
- Blade / API hybrid
- PostgreSQL database
- WhatsApp integration (FonnteHelper)
- External ERP integration (KledoService)

========================
ROOT STRUCTURE
========================

/
├── app/
│   ├── Http/Controllers/
│   ├── Services/ (KledoService, ERP Integration)
│   ├── Models/ (Order, Product, Customer, Invoice, Driver, Supplier)
│   └── Helpers/ (FonnteHelper WhatsApp)
├── database/migrations/
├── resources/views/
├── routes/
│   ├── web.php
│   └── api.php
├── start.sh
├── start-production.sh
└── Dockerfile
